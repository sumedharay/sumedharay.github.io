Welcome!

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Economics at the University of Oklahoma, specializing in labor economics. My research focuses on political economy and socio-economic outcomes, particularly the effects of communal violence on minority groups in India.

I primarily use econometric methods, such as difference-in-differences (DiD) analysis, to analyze large datasets and draw policy-relevant insights. Through my work, I aim to contribute to discussions on governance, equity, and development.
